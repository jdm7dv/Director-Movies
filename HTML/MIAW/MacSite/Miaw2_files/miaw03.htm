Advantages of an MIAW over a movie cast member

Director converts an imported movie into a film loop, which loses some of the movie's features. Displaying a movie as an MIAW has several important advantages:

Lingo in an MIAW is much more robust.
The MIAW and the main movie can communicate and send each other instructions.
Window types and sizes can be controlled.
